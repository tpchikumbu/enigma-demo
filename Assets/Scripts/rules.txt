# The Game of 25:

1. **Objective**: Be the player to reach exactly 25 points.

2. **Starting**: The game begins at 0 points.

3. **Turns**: Players alternate turns.

4. **Making a Move**: On your turn, add either 1, 2, or 3 points to the total.
    Ice = 1
    Fire = 2
    BlackHole = 3

5. **Winning**: The first player to reach exactly 25 points wins the game.
